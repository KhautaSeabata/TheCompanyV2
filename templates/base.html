üÉè Khasino Game Rules

üìã Overview

Khasino is the traditional South African Casino card game played with a 40-card pack (standard 52-card pack with all face cards removed). The game can be played by 2, 3, or 4 players.

üéØ Objective

The entire objective is to capture as many points as possible during gameplay. Point distribution depends on the number of players:

  • 2 players: 11 total points available
  • 3 players: 7 total points available
  • 4 players (singles): 7 total points available

üíé Points System

  • Most cards: 2 points (1 point each if tied)
  • Most spades: 2 points (1 point each if tied)
  • Two of spades ("spy two"): 1 point
  • Ten of diamonds ("mummy"): 2 points
  • Each Ace: 1 point

Note: In 7-point games, most cards and most spades are not counted.

üéÆ Gameplay

Card Dealing:

  • 2 players: 10 cards each per round
  • 3 players: 13 cards each, 1 card face up on table
  • 4 players: 10 cards each

Actions on Your Turn:

  • Capture: Play a card to capture single cards or builds
  • Build: Create a combination of cards with a specific sum
  • Change Build: Modify an opponent's build (if you can capture the new value)
  • Discard: Play a card to the table without capturing

üèóÔ∏è Building Rules

Simple Build: A pile of cards whose value equals their sum (max 10). You must have a card in hand that can capture this build.

Compound Build: Multiple sets of cards, each adding to the same value. Cannot be changed once created.
